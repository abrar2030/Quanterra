About Me

I am a highly motivated and passionate software developer with a strong foundation in computer science and financial engineering. With experience in backend development, cloud technologies, and automation, I thrive in environments that challenge me to solve complex problems. My expertise spans across Java, Python, and various DevOps tools, enabling me to build scalable and efficient applications that drive business success.

Throughout my career, I have worked on projects involving cloud computing, machine learning, big data processing, and cybersecurity. At Ericsson, I developed data-driven applications, optimized data pipelines, and applied machine learning for anomaly detection. My experience with AWS, Kubernetes, and Terraform has further strengthened my ability to deploy and manage cloud-native applications effectively.

In addition to my industry experience, I have contributed to academia as a Teaching Assistant, mentoring students in Python, Operating Systems, and Algorithms & Data Structures. I am deeply passionate about knowledge-sharing, innovation, and continuous learning, and I actively seek opportunities to expand my expertise in artificial intelligence, deep learning, and cybersecurity.

My educational background includes a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Eötvös Loránd University and a Master’s in Financial Engineering from WorldQuant University. This unique combination allows me to integrate advanced computing techniques with financial analytics, making me well-suited for roles that involve quantitative finance, risk modeling, and algorithmic trading.

My long-term vision is to contribute to the future of AI-driven financial systems, develop innovative technologies that enhance security and efficiency in cloud environments, and work on projects that revolutionize the way businesses operate. I believe in continuous growth, adaptability, and pushing beyond limits to achieve excellence in every endeavor.
